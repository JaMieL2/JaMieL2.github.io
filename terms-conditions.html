ABOUT US

Instant Flight is a high-tech traffic company. We are trying to provide human a faster and more convenient travel way.

OPPORTUNITY

With the rapid growth of population and vehicles, traffic congestion has become one of the serious problems in metropolises. Instant Flight focuses on the fast and comfortable travel needs of metropolis entrepreneurs. It creates a fast and sustainable regional transportation mode.

PRODUCT

1.Aircraft selling

We has created the eVTOL(electric vertical take-off and landing) small commercial aircraft, JK-03. Instant Flight will sell JK-03 which is low price, low operation cost and high efficiency and range to airlines.

2. Travel service

JK-03 providing high efficient, comfortable and quiet travels while fulfill airworthiness requirements and standard redundancy needs. It is safe and reliable, bringing users a new zero-emission air travel.

INNOVATION INTRODUCTION

Product innovation concept: JK-03 eVTOL small commercial aircraft is committed to solving the fast and comfortable travel needs of business people in metropolis, and creating a fast and sustainable regional three-dimensional air traffic mode.

Product innovative design: The appearance imitates the manta rays fish shape, which is beautiful and fashionable, and greatly improves the practicability and functionality of the wing area.

Product innovation technology:

● hydrogen fuel cell combined with solar cell energy system, zero carbon emission, environmental protection and energy conservation, and energy expenditure cost is reduced by 25%;

● noise reducing tilted ducted fan, which can control the noise at 45 dB and tilt 90 degrees to take off and land vertically and quickly at any site.

● 3D air traffic network is an intelligent three-dimensional traffic network service platform in line with urban air mobility.

Product innovation services: diversified service forms, integrating fast travel, charter flights, cargo transportation and sightseeing services together.

COMPETITIVE ADVANTAGES

The main competitors of instant flight are three listed companies using lithium battery which are "Yihang" from China, "Lilium" from Germany, and the "Joby" from U.S.

JK-03 uses hydrogen energy combined with solar energy, which is more energy-saving and environment-friendly, with shorter charging time, larger passenger capacity, stronger range and higher cost performance;

GO-TO-MARKET

1.Aircraft selling

The selling price of the finished aircraft is 50% of that of the same category abroad

2.Travel service

The ticket price of the first year's route operation is sold at a discount of 60% of the price of online special vehicles to quickly accumulate customers and then operate customers.

Create creative advertisements and spread all kinds of promotion channels online and offline.

Invite KOL of target customer group to carry out trial ride experience for promotion.

Formulate membership, discount, points and other systems to attract users.
