Three-dimensional Traffic Networks

Instant Flight will establish in Guangzhou as the center point, and within a radius of 200 kilometers, our flights will connect the core cities in the Big Bay Area in China. We will build airports and Instant Flight Three-dimensional Traffic Networks between urban and suburban communities, transportation stations, and business centers. There is a huge crowd of people who need short-distance air flight services in this area. Our ticket price is similar to taxi and Uber prices.

We designed 10 aircrafts through 10 months and successfully verify the configuration. We believe that every persistence in difficulties brings us closer to success!
